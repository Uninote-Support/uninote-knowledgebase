---
title: How can I change my account name?
---

<Tip>You can change your name at any time from the account settings. Changes will be reflected immediately.</Tip>


## A. You can change it at any time from the account settings.

### Steps

1. Click on the account icon in the upper right corner of the screen.
2. Open [My Account].<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (29).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Click [Edit] and save.<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (30).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<Note>You can change your name at any time from the account settings. Changes will be reflected immediately.</Note>
